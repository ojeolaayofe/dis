Overview Of Network
Security Cameras

A popular question we have frequently received at DeployedItSolution is “Are security cameras connected to a network?” In, this article, we aim to discuss network security cameras and the role of a Network Technician. This article also succinctly discusses the merits of cameras and highlights a few recommendations for businesses. Read with me, don’t miss out!

In this new age of technology, businesses can now employ the use of video surveillance technology to serve as a crime deterrent. Several decades ago, Security cameras were usually placed in a dormant position which is not fully efficient as a tool for monitoring business assets. In recent times, these obsolete cameras are now being replaced with modern sophisticated Cameras such as Network Security Cameras or simply called IP Cameras.

It is the job of a Network Technician to install and configure these cameras. A Network Technician further analyse and troubleshoots these cameras when they are at fault. Furthermore, images, recordings, and videos recovered from these security cameras are seamlessly deployed on the cloud with the expertise of a Network Technician. The data received such as footage are often streamlined through a network so that it may be accessible when you need them.

The security feeds can also be remotely retrieved and accessed in real-time simultaneously from different locations and multiple users thereby providing a more enhanced, comfortable and flexible way of monitoring your businesses or other physical properties. Hence, the use of IP Cameras is a reliable security tool for your business and as such highly recommended.

Using IP Cameras does not require many resources, all that is needed include the camera(s), network cables, a network video recorder, and some specific network devices. However, the installation part is a very tedious task and may require the services of professionals.

Benefits of Network Security Cameras

Do you agree with me that the protection of personal and business assets has received more attention in the past decade? Therefore, IP Cameras are a must-have tool for emerging businesses. Perhaps, this is a significant reason for the rapid increase in the jobs of Network Technicians. Here are some of the benefits of installing Network Security or IP Cameras for your business.

  • There is no limit to the usage of IP Cameras that can be installed in any of your business outdoor or indoor spaces.
  • IP Network Systems offer better and high-quality resolutions since they use modern technology.
  • With the use of the Camera’s IP addresses, you can easily access camera footage of your business place remotely from anywhere in the world.
  • IP cameras do not only recognize objects and persons, they also detect motions and can even be automated to produce warning sounds whenever there is a likely breach.
  • Configuration is not expensive and takes no time which makes it faster for you to start seeing what happens exactly when it happens.
  • Network Security Cameras can be deployed to be compatible with existing network systems that are already configured in your business space.
  • IP cameras are manufactured to ideally comply with regulatory standards such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
  • Network Security Cameras have been tested to be durable and they last for a long time. They are also resistant to water, spillages, rust, and fire.
  • They are automatically enabled to remain functional during the night and in total darkness.
  • They are equipped with sufficient storage capacity and can also be deployed to the cloud storage as well.
